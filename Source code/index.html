About Us

Who are we?

Our agency, which started operating in 2020, has signed successful projects with its professional and dynamic team, adopting quality as a principle. Our agency, which has made a lot of talk about the stars it has brought to the sector since its establishment, is proud of being a pioneer in making the first signatures in their careers by bringing many young people to their professions every year. With all these achievements, our agency has reached an international level; It has become the number one choice of producers with its series, movies, commercial actors and models.

Our mission

As Powerpuff Casting, our mission is to provide the industry with names at international standards, while continuing to meet the needs of producers, directors, casting directors and related companies at the highest quality, and to ensure that our actors and models adopt the team spirit, disciplined, smiling faces and adopt customer satisfaction with their image of quality.

Our vision

Our aim is to keep acting and modeling professions one step ahead every day, without departing from our position and trust, and to be your solution partner in your advertisements and promotions by using our opportunities and creating opportunities, and to make you enjoy working with us in your promotion and organization works.

Why you should choose us?

As Powerpuff Casting, we have come to this point with the efforts we made on this road we set out to get high marks. Our only wish is that our beloved teacher will not remain indifferent to our efforts.
